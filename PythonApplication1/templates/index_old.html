Ask your Hands

In early infancy, through our hands, we learn to understand and “see”
the world. Much of human activity requires and revolves around the use
of our hands. We have chosen to concentrate our efforts on designing
and implementing a device for aiding users with a common problem:
tracking a limited number of personal objects and monitoring activities.
We have implemented and tested a small unobtrusive wrist-worn device,
which records the movement of the wrist with an inertial sensor and creates a
wrist-centric video stream of the hand and fingers and their immediate environment.
